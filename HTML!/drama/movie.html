Keeravani Production company DVV Entertainment Distributed by Pen Studios (North India) Lyca Productions (Tamil Nadu) KVN Productions (Karnataka) HR Pictures (Kerala) Release date 25 March 2022 Running time 182 minutes[1][2] Country India Language Telugu Budget ₹550 crore[3][4] Box office ₹1,387.26 crore[5][6] RRR[note 1] (subtitled onscreen as Roudram Ranam Rudhiram) is a 2022 Indian Telugu-language epic period action drama film directed by S. S. Rajamouli, who co-wrote the film with V. Vijayendra Prasad. It was produced by D. V. V. Danayya of DVV Entertainment. The film stars N. T. Rama Rao Jr. and Ram Charan alongside an ensemble cast with Ajay Devgn, Alia Bhatt, Shriya Saran, Samuthirakani, Ray Stevenson, Alison Doody, and Olivia Morris playing supporting roles. It is a historical fiction film of two Indian revolutionaries, Alluri Sitarama Raju (Charan) and Komaram Bheem (Rama Rao), their friendship, and their fight against the British Raj.+
